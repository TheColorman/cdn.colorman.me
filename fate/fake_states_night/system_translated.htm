The lost Saber class and the truth behind the warped Holy Grail?!

Are you prepared to learn the truth behind everything?

 

An explanation of the game mechanics,

by Muda Makoto.

 

It's completely unrelated to it (or so we say); it was never supposed to be made public;

it's the worst sort of homage to that famous work, Fate/stay night; and it's now under development!

Its name is...

Fake/states night


A new Holy Grail War, held in the enigmatic American city of Snowfield

Watch, as the warped Heroic Spirits battle!

 

 

The main character... is you!?

The game is text-based, kind of like ***dou 2! If the "2" was a "1", we'd have to censor that too, and then none of this would make any sense!

You could say that it's like Fate/holl*w ataraxia, except that instead of looping, all the choices are available to you from the beginning!

Be careful, though - even though you're the one making the choices, that doesn't mean you'll be the main character at the center of the plot. Depending on how things turn out, the Holy Grail War could end with you playing the role of Innocent Bystander A!

The map screen is under development!

It will take you 15-30 minutes to move from place to place. While you're moving, time will be flowing at every location, and if an event occurs, you'll enter sound novel mode!

● The five Heroic Spirits that protect you

To fill the gap left by the loss of the Saber class, you are able to use the power of five Heroic Spirits! Perseus, Jason, Scathach, the Hydra, and more! From ten or so different types of Servants, take your pick of five!

 In a single game, you have just 30 minutes for which you can summon your Heroic Spirits!
Each Heroic Spirit is powerful enough to stand on equal footing with any other Servant. You can summon them all at once, or you can summon each one separately, for just five minutes at a time. If you carefully consider the situation in Snowfield, and select the best, most suitable Servants, you could even attain the Holy Grail!

● Summon them all!


In this game, Gilgamesh remains the most powerful servant. Nonetheless, if you catch him with his guard down and use your Command Spells to summon all five of your Servants at once, ...?

● The ultimate weapon: a Noble Phantasm


Each Servant possesses a "Noble Phantasm", a special move of sorts.
If you activate a Noble Phantasm without making any errors, you are certain to achieve victory. However, not only do different Noble Phantasms have distinct affinities; they also require much magical energy. If you overuse Noble Phantasms, the mere 30 minutes for which you can materialize your Servants will decrease yet further. Before using a Noble Phantasm, you should check the status screen to make sure you have enough magical energy.

・ How many Noble Phantasms does Perseus have?!


Normally, a Servant has one or two Noble Phantasms. But you can summon Perseus, who can use the bag Kibisis; a helm to conceal himself; the serpent-slaying scythe Harpe; and so on and so forth - five in all! Since you won't be able to use them all in the 30-minute time limit, it's very important that you choose wisely!

● Alliance or hostility?!


All of the many Masters and Servants have their quirks. In this battle royale, you can't fight head-on and hope to win. From time to time, you'll have to form temporary alliances!

 ・ Alignment system
You can't make an alliance with just anybody. What's important are the alignments of your Servants. Lawful, Neutral, Chaotic; Good, Neutral, Evil. Different goals and personalities for all the Servants. But even if they coincide with the player, will it really be that easy to form an alliance?! Even if you do form an alliance, if your goals differ, you could easily be betrayed, so be careful!
・ And what about hiding for a while...?
There's more to life than battle. If the player so desires, he can spend the entire week in hiding, not summoning a single Servant.

● Hidden items

in Snowfield?!


Somebody has a hidden purpose for this Holy Grail War. Perhaps for that reason, there are valuable items hidden all over the place. If you use these items well, you might be able to make progress without expending the time you have available to summon your Servant...

 ・ A puppet's arm
It looks like a true human arm, through and through, and yet it is artificial. If you successfully connect it to your body, it seems that you can touch spiritual beings. They say it was made by a puppeteer from Japan, but...?

・Mana compass
A compass that points in the direction of the strongest magical energy. If you use it, you'll easily be able to locate hidden Servants. The name of the former owner, "Rin Tohsaka", is inscribed on its back.

・ Fighting alongside Berserker?
At times, you will receive a request to help someone else in battle. The Berserker known as Jack the Ripper and his Master seem like trustworthy folk at first glance, but...?

 

・ Rider's plague
The calamities brought by Rider will spread through all of Snowfield. Rider saps people of their magical energy and worse still, brings about these disasters. How can Rider be defeated?!


● Eating people to absorb their magical energy?!


Servants deplete their magical energy gauge with every action they take. Combat takes a great toll; the use of a Noble Phantasm takes an even greater toll.
The magical energy gauge replenishes by itself, but if you exhaust your gauge against an enemy Servant, you may have to resort to that worst of options, eating people! Ordinarily, only Servants with either the Chaotic or Evil alignments will choose to do so, but if the Master so desires, he can force his Servant to eat, using a Command Spell!

If you absorb enough magical energy, you might be able to summon your Servants for a longer period of time! But be careful - you might end up forcing your Servant to change alignments.

・ Heroic Spirits at the hot springs!


There are hot springs in Snowfield. Ordinarily, female servants like Scathach, Scylla, and Dong Bai wouldn't want to bathe in the hot springs, but if you use a Command Spell.... If you get too engrossed in it, you might exhaust your 30 minutes just sitting there, you know?
